Mineral Assemblage Gibbs Energy Minimization		  
============================================
                                          
C routine to compute a stable mineralogical assemblage using Gibbs Free Energy Minimization. MAGEMin  combines discretization of the equations of state in composition space (Connolly, 1990) with linear programming (de Capitani & Brown,1987), and extends the mass constrained Gibbs-hyperplane rotation (Piro et al., 2013) method to account for the mixing-on-sites that takes place in silicate mineral solid solutions (Riel et al., *submitted*).
                    
- Igneous thermodynamic database (Holland et al., 2008)
- KNCFMASHTOCr chemical space                           
- Solid solutions (biotite, clinopyroxene, cordierite, epidote, fluids, garnet, hornblende, ilmenite, K-felspar, liquid, muscovite, olivine, orthopyroxene, plagioclase, spinel)
